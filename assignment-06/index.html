Bannana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup butter
  • 3/4 Cup surgar
  • 3 Teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3 Mashed bannanas
  • 1 Teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt
  • 2 Cups flour

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF.
  2. Mix together butter, sugar, vanilla, and eggs
  3. Add the mashed bannanas
  4. Mix in salt, baking soda, and flour
  5. Bake for 65 minutes
